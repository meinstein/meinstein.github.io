My name is Maximillian Einstein.1 I am an Angelino living in Amsterdam.2 Profesionally, I am a software engineer with a passion for connecting dots.3 However, I arrived at this occupation by nontraditional means.4 In terms of graduate education, I have a JD/MBA dual degree5 and am a licensed member of The State Bar of California.6 In terms of undergraduate education, I have a dual B.A. in disparate areas of social science.7 I also fluently speak a quirky German dialect8 and have the fluffient pup in the world.9
